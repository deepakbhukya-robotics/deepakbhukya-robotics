<h2 align="center">About</h2>

I am an **Engineering Management graduate and Mechatronics Engineer** building at the intersection of **robotics, embedded software, automation, product development, data analytics, and technical project execution**.

My experience spans autonomous rover systems, hydrogen fuel-cell integration, sensor-based navigation, IoT monitoring, electrical and electronics design, machine-learning projects, engineering simulation, web interfaces, product discovery, and cross-functional program coordination.

At the University of Maryland Baltimore County, I helped coordinate an autonomous hydrogen fuel-cell rover program involving **290+ students and 70+ multidisciplinary teams**. My work included embedded programming, circuit integration, motor control, sensor validation, wireless data transmission, technical documentation, design reviews, troubleshooting, competition logistics, and prototype-readiness planning.

I approach engineering with a **product mindset**: understand the user, define measurable requirements, evaluate technical risk, build reliable systems, validate performance, document decisions, and continuously improve execution.

My software experience includes **Python, Java, C, C++, SQL, JavaScript, MATLAB, Arduino development, machine learning, data analysis, database applications, and frontend dashboards**. I am especially interested in applying software and data to physical products, manufacturing systems, robotics platforms, and operational workflows.
